HOLLOW KNIGHT

What is it?

Hollow Knight is an action adventure video game developed in Unity by Team Cherry which released in 2017. The game starts simply with little exposition. All you know is that you are a bug, and you must explore. After a brief tutorial level, you find a small town named Dirtmouth with only one inhabitant, Elderbug, who explains that they don't see many visitors these days; but the few they do see are only passing through before they descend into the expansive caverns below. Bugs descend for a variety of reasons, like treasure or adventure, or for some simply because they feel a calling. Our blank-faced protagonist gives no insight towards their motivation before they themselves leave the comfort of Dirtmouth and descend to the darkness below.

The game has an average play time of 27 hours, but the current world record for an Any% glitchless run is 31m 24s.

The World and Lore of Hollownest

The world of Hallownest was once occupied by bugs of different factions; but everything changed with the arrival of the Pale King, the reincarnation of a felled wyrm. Wyrms are higher beings and are capable of not only coherent thought, but a certain level of precognition. The Pale King came to Hallownest with the goal of uniting this land under one kingdom. Some lands knelt before others. The Mosskin of Greenpath and the Mushroom Clan of the Fungal Wastes admired the kings intelligence and pledged allegiance immediately. Within these same wastes lived the Mantis Tribe. While they value intelligence, they far preferred their own autonomy. The Pale King struck a deal that they main remain sovereign if they kept the Spider Tribe of Deepnest at bay from invading the rest of Hallownest. However, gaining the following of the Moth Tribe would prove to be the downfall of The Pale King.

The Moth Tribe worshipped the god Radiance. They quickly flocked to the Pale King. Soon Radiance began to be forgotten but before that could happen they infected the minds of the bugs of Hallownest through the dream realm. This infection made empty husks of the bugs that had once been granted higher thought by the Pale King. The subjects of the Pale King tried everything to combat this infection, but with no success. The Pale Kings came up with a plan. He fathered hundreds of children and threw the clutch into the abyss below the City of Tears. Here lived a sinister element they only called Void. The Pale King hoped for a perfect child to hatch who was filled with Void. Within this vessel the Pale King hoped to trap Radiance and contain the source of this infection.
